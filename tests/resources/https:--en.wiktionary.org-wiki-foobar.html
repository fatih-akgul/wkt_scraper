English[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
foobar
Wikipedia

Etymology[edit]

Phonetic spelling of FUBAR, which is either an acronym for "Fucked up beyond all recognition", or derived from foo.[1]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈfuːˌbɑː(ɹ)/
  • Audio (AU)(file)

Noun[edit]

foobar (plural foobars)

  1. (slang) A serious mistake.
  2. (programming) A metasyntactic variable name, a place holder for words; compare foo, bar.

  • foo fighter
  • FUBAR
