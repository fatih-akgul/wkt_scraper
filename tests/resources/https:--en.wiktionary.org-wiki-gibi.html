Portuguese[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Brazil) IPA(key): /ʒiˈbi/

Noun[edit]

gibi m (plural gibis)

  1. (Brazil, slang) comics; comic book

Related terms[edit]

  • revista em quadrinhos

Turkish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Ottoman Turkish گبی‎ (gibi), from Proto-Turkic *kēpi (“like”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɟibi/

Postposition[edit]

gibi

  1. like (similar to)
    Annen gibi konuşuyorsun.
    You are talking like your mother.
  2. such as
    Vietnam, Kamboçya ve Laos gibi Asya ülkeleri…
    Asian countries such as Vietnam, Cambodia and Laos…
